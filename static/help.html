User Manual

BadQuiz is a quiz for everyone who wants to have some quick fun. The questions are generated at each time the quiz is played. This ensures that the quiz never gets reptional and boring. We have used an aesthetically pleasing and simplistic design to help in creating a usable and practical experience, and as such much of the functionality built into the website will come naturally to the end user, with them not having to consult the user manual.


Hardware requirements

To play the quiz all you need is a device (personal computer, tablet or a mobile phone) that is connected to the Internet. You will also need a recent version of your favourite browser.

Internet Explorer version 10. or newer,

Firefox version 66. or newer,

Chrome version 72 or newer,

Safari 11 or newer.

Home page

To access the BadQuiz website, simply type badquiz.co.uk in the address bar,then press enter or search and the BadQuiz home page will open.

Picture 1: BadQuiz home page.

At the home page there are a couple of different options you can choose from. You can log in, register, check the leaderboards, play without logging in or open the User manual / Help.


Registration

Picture 2: BadQuiz registration page.

If you to be part of our leaderboards, you will have to make an account on the BadQuiz website. There are three different options of making an account:

  1. Using the BadQuiz registration form. You have to enter your first name, last name, email and set up a password that follows the rules written under the “Password” field: Password must be minimum 8 characters and must contain at least one uppercase letter, lowercase letter and a number

So an example of a valid password is “BadQuiz2019”.

  1. Using your Google account. Simply press and choose your Google account you would like to register with.

  2. Using your Facebook account. Click on button and continue as (your Facebook profile first name).


After you register successfully, you will be redirected to the home page.


Login

Picture 3: BadQuiz login page.

When you visit the website next time, after registration, you are most likely not going to be logged in. But you do not have to make a new profile again. You can simply click on the “Login” button on the home page and you will be directed to the page shown in Picture 3. As in the registration page, you have three options of logging in: using an existing Facebook, Google or BadQuiz profile. You should log in using the same method you used when registering. Google and Facebook buttons can be used on the login page to log in as well as to register.


Playing the quiz

Picture 4: Playing BadQuiz.

The quiz can be played as a guest, without a BadQuiz profile or logged in. The benefit of playing logged in is that the players can see how well they are performing compared to other people. To start playing you can go to the homepage (Picture 1) and click on the “Play” button or you can click on the “Play” button on the navigation bar on the top of the screen.

After clicking on the “Play” button the game will start automatically. Above the questions there is question number that shows what is the total number of questions and what is the number of the question that is being answered at the moment. To the right of the question numbering element is the current score window. Each question is worth 10 points and whenever you answer the question correctly, 10 points are added to your score. The timer is placed under the questions. For each question you have between 5 and 15 seconds to answer the question before that questions is skipped and marked as incorrectly answered.

After answering a question, the background of the answer you chose will turn green if the answer was correct or red if it was wrong with the correct answer being green, as shown in the examples(Picture 5 and Picture 6) below..
